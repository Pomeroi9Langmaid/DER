DER asked

DER currently uses the listed tags and would like to understand whether they can be supported. DER is open to extending the tag structure if needed.

Several tags are directly available or closely mapped. Others need closer alignment depending on whether they are operational filters, editorial/profile tags, accessibility attributes, event categories, or tour/activity/provider content.

Tour/activity note: Smartvel can support content classification and visibility where available, but does not provide booking functionality. If bookable tours or activities are part of the requirement, DER's external provider feeds or APIs may need to be considered.

3. Ideal use case: hotel geodata and nearby content Supported, configuration needed

DER asked

DER would ideally provide the geodata of a hotel and receive content assigned to that specific location or nearby area.

Smartvel response

Yes, this is conceptually aligned with Smartvel's POI API. The hotel location can act as the anchor point for nearby POI and destination content.

Accor reference: similar location-based use case

Take a look at our new client Accor and the travel guide experience Smartvel created. The part closest to what DER is trying to achieve is the map-based hotel context, where hotels are shown together with surrounding POI-style destination content.
