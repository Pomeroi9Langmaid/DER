DER requested information

Question-by-question response

DER asked

Data coverage: destinations available and granularity.

Smartvel response

See the attached Destination Coverage tab, filterable by regional area, country and destination. Smartvel coverage is structured at destination-city level, with POI-level content including name, description, image/image reference, geodata and tags.

DER asked

Provide hotel geodata and receive nearby content.

Smartvel response

Yes, this is conceptually aligned with our POI API. The hotel location can act as the anchor point for nearby POI and destination content, subject to defining radius, coverage, content types, languages and location logic.

DER asked

Additional access or activation for Crete, Mallorca and Turkey.

Smartvel response

Smartvel is reviewing the most suitable destination-city/API test options for the requested markets. Recommended test locations and access/activation details will be confirmed separately once validated. For Turkey, the specific destination city should be confirmed.

DER asked

Rough cost indication.

Smartvel response

A rough indication can be provided once destination scope, languages, API-only vs widget/API support, hotel-location logic, tag/filter needs and any tour/activity/event provider feed requirements are confirmed.

DER asked

API documentation and next steps.

Smartvel response

API documentation links are provided below. Recommended next step is to align the POI data/API requirement and the separate Trip Planner + Explore & Go POC track.
