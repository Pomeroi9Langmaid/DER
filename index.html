DER request 3

Ideal use case: hotel geodata and nearby content ? Supported, configuration needed

DER asked

DER would ideally provide the geodata of a hotel and receive content assigned to that specific location or nearby area.

Smartvel response

Yes, this is conceptually aligned with Smartvel’s POI API. The hotel location can act as the anchor point for nearby POI and destination content.

To define this properly, Smartvel would need to align the expected radius, destination coverage, content types, required languages, and the geographic matching rule to apply.

Configuration pointNeeded from DER
Radius / nearby logicConfirm whether matching should be radius-based, destination-city based, or another geographic rule
Content typesConfirm whether this should include POI/editorial content only or also tours/events/activities
LanguagesConfirm required languages
Destination scopeConfirm destination cities and priority markets
