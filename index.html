Tags requiring closer alignment: SUITABLE_FOR_SOLO, SUITABLE_FOR_GROUP, SUITABLE_FOR_WHEELCHAIRS, PRIVATE_TOUR, SMALL_GROUP, DAY_TRIPS, CLASSES and LIVE_MUSIC. These depend on whether DER needs strict operational filters, softer editorial/profile tags or future extensions.
